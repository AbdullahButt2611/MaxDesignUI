About us
Our Story
Umair Umar founded their firm in 2007, after completing their respective educations in the fields of Architecture and Interior Design. He established his business in Lahore-Max Designs which has harvested a reputation acros Pakistan particularly in Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, Multan, Sialkot, Faisalabad, Gujranwala and also expanding its roots in the global markets.

For most of the projects, contrast is key throughout his careful selections both in terms of materials and colors. What remains consistent throughout; is the use of aesthetics: Sustainable and innovative contemporary architecture, Neo classic, classical designs. With more than 15 year’s experience, we are well known due to our work in Pakistan and international markets, an achievement that is attributable to his unique business model, which forms the foundation for our strong, dynamic corporate strategy.
